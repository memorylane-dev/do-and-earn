# Privacy Policy — Do & Earn (할거다하면)

This is a draft Privacy Policy for App Store review.
Publish it at a public URL (e.g., GitHub Pages, public Notion page) and register the URL in App Store Connect.

---

**Do & Earn** (also known as 할거다하면, referred to as the "Service") is a mobile application provided by Shinhye Lee (referred to as the "Operator"). This policy explains how the Service collects, uses, stores, and deletes personal information.

Effective date: 2026-04-19

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Sign-in

When you sign in with an Apple or Google account, we receive the following information from the provider:

- Account identifier (Apple sub / Google sub)
- Email address (if you choose "Hide My Email" with Apple, a relay address is stored)
- Name (optional — Apple provides this only on first sign-in, and you may choose not to share it)

### Information You Enter

- Todo (task) titles
- Reward (coupon) titles and point costs
- Shared relationship labels
- Subject, message, and reply email entered through the in-app contact form

### Usage Records

- Point-changing events such as completing/undoing a todo or redeeming/undoing a reward
- Shared relationship invite, accept, and disconnect history
- Subscription status and expiration dates

### Payment

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, passwords, or any payment method details. Subscription payments are processed through Apple In-App Purchase. We only receive transaction identifiers, product IDs, and subscription validity information from Apple.

### Information We Do Not Collect

- Location data
- Contacts, calendar, photos, or other on-device data
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA) or tracking data
- Microphone, camera, or other sensor data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Identifying your account and maintaining your session
- Providing core features: todos, rewards, shared spaces, and point balances
- Verifying subscription status and granting premium access
- Receiving and responding to support inquiries
- Preventing misuse

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

We delete personal information without delay once its purpose has been fulfilled.

**When you delete your account:**
- You can request account deletion from Profile > Delete Account in the app.
- Your account becomes immediately unusable and personal information is deleted or anonymized.
- Payment and subscription records may be retained for the period required by applicable law.
- Shared spaces are moved to an "ended" state so that your partner's records are not lost. The deleted user's identifying information is anonymized.

## 4. Sharing With Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law enforcement through legally mandated procedures.

## 5. External Services

We use the following external services to operate the Service. Your data may be stored and processed on their servers (United States):

- **Apple** — Sign-in, payment, subscription management
- **Google** — Sign-in
- **Railway** — Server hosting
- **Supabase** — Database

## 6. Your Rights

- You may request to view, correct, or delete your personal information.
- You can exercise these rights through Profile > Delete Account or Profile > Contact in the app.

## 7. Security

- All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.
- We do not collect passwords directly — we use Apple/Google OAuth for authentication.
- We do not use advertising tracking and do not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

## 8. Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will notify you through in-app notice or on the page where this policy is published.
